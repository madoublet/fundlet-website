What's inside

A complete fundraising shop, beautiful out of the box.

Everything you need to launch, run, and track a fundraiser — built right into the store you already have.

01 — Landing pages

Amazing landing pages

The key to a great fundraiser is building excitement. Fundlet pages look professional the moment you publish — no designer required.

  • Upload campaign photos and your story
  • Live progress bar tracking sales toward a goal
  • Customize with your own product collections
02 — Onboarding

Built-in onboarding

Get started fast with built-in forms to bring on new campaigns and participants — no spreadsheets, no manual labor.

  • Add campaigns directly from your homepage
  • Share one link to onboard participants
  • Works on any device, fast and efficient
Onboarding form
03 — Tracking

Simple tracking

Tag and track every sale by campaign and participant, then pay out with confidence. The numbers are always at your fingertips.

  • Track sales down to the individual participant
  • A dashboard that keeps progress in view
  • Exportable reports, powered by order tags
