Fundlet is a Shopify app that lets small businesses run product fundraisers directly from their online store. No third-party platform, no manual order tracking — everything lives inside Shopify.

Whether you sell popcorn, coffee, candy, or baked goods, Fundlet turns your existing products into a fundraising program that schools, sports teams, churches, and community organizations can run on your behalf.

---

## How It Works

1. **Install Fundlet** from the Shopify App Store — one click, no code required.
2. **Create a campaign** — set a fundraising goal, upload a photo, and pick which products to include.
3. **Onboard participants** — share a single signup link and let participants register themselves.
4. **Watch the sales come in** — orders flow through your normal Shopify checkout. Track progress by campaign and participant in real time.
5. **Export and pay out** — close the campaign and export reports to calculate payouts.

## Why Shopify Merchants Choose Fundlet

Fundlet is built specifically for merchants who already have a Shopify store and want to offer fundraising as a service to their community. Unlike generic fundraising platforms, Fundlet uses your actual products, your actual checkout, and your actual fulfillment workflow — so there's nothing new to learn and no duplicate data to manage.

[Install Fundlet on Shopify](https://apps.shopify.com/fundlet) and run your first fundraiser in minutes.
