Baked goods fundraisers are beloved for a reason — everyone buys them, and the products feel special. With Fundlet, your bakery can offer fundraising programs to schools, PTAs, sports teams, and community groups directly through your Shopify store, with automated tracking and no manual paperwork.

---

## Built for Product-Based Fundraising

Fundlet is designed for Shopify merchants who sell physical products — exactly like a bakery. Your existing products become the fundraising catalog. You pick which items to include in a campaign, set a goal, and Fundlet handles the rest.

- Participants sign up and get a personal sharing link
- Supporters choose from your products and check out through Shopify
- Every order is tagged by campaign and participant automatically
- You bake, fulfill, and ship just like a normal order

---

## Pre-Order Fundraisers

Bakeries often run pre-order style fundraisers — take all the orders first, then bake in bulk. Fundlet works naturally with this model because orders flow through Shopify's standard checkout. You can close a campaign at a date of your choosing and fulfill all orders at once.

---

## Run Multiple School Campaigns Simultaneously

If you work with multiple schools or organizations at the same time, Fundlet manages them all from a single Shopify admin. Each campaign has its own goal tracker, participant list, and landing page — no juggling separate spreadsheets.
