Flowers are a natural fundraising product — they're a gift people are already planning to buy, which makes them an easy sell for any participant. With Fundlet, your flower shop or floral studio can run fundraising campaigns for schools, PTAs, sports teams, and community groups directly through your Shopify store.

---

## Built for Seasonal Campaigns

Floral fundraisers peak around Mother's Day, Valentine's Day, and spring — some of the highest-volume gifting periods of the year. Fundlet lets you spin up a campaign quickly to capture that demand:

- Create a campaign in minutes with your seasonal arrangements or pre-designed bundles
- Set a fundraising goal and open it to participants right away
- Supporters order online and you fulfill on your normal schedule
- Close the campaign before your key fulfillment date and process orders in one batch

---

## Pre-Order Fundraisers Work Perfectly

Florists often work best with advance orders rather than on-demand fulfillment. Fundlet's campaign structure is ideal for this — you collect all orders during the campaign window, then fulfill everything when the campaign closes. No surprise orders, no scrambled inventory.

---

## Every Organization Gets Its Own Page

Each campaign gets its own landing page with a custom photo and goal tracker, so the school or team feels like the fundraiser was designed just for them. You manage all active campaigns from a single view in your Shopify admin.
