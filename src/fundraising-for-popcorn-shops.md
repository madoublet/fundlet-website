Popcorn is one of the most popular fundraising products there is — but running a popcorn fundraiser has traditionally meant paper order forms, cash collection, and hours of manual tallying. Fundlet replaces all of that with a Shopify-native fundraising experience that runs itself.

With Fundlet, your popcorn shop can offer professional fundraising campaigns to schools, sports leagues, churches, and clubs, all through your existing Shopify store.

---

## Replace Paper Order Forms for Good

Traditional popcorn fundraisers require each participant to carry a paper form, collect cash or checks, and hand everything back to an organizer who manually adds it all up. Fundlet works the opposite way:

- Participants get a personal link to share with friends and family
- Supporters order directly through your Shopify store
- Every order is automatically tagged by campaign and participant — no spreadsheet needed
- You fulfill orders through your existing Shopify workflow

---

## Built for Multiple Campaigns at Once

If your popcorn shop works with several schools or organizations at a time, Fundlet handles that too. Each campaign gets its own landing page, goal tracker, and participant list — all managed from your Shopify admin.
