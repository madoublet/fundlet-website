Chocolate is one of the original fundraising products — easy to sell, universally loved, and a natural gift for any occasion. With Fundlet, your chocolate shop can run professional fundraising campaigns for schools, sports teams, churches, and community groups directly through your existing Shopify store.

---

## From Paper Order Forms to Online Orders

Traditional chocolate fundraisers rely on participants carrying catalogs and collecting cash or checks. Fundlet moves the entire process online without changing how you operate:

- Each participant gets a unique link to share with their network
- Supporters order directly through your Shopify store
- Orders are automatically tagged by campaign and participant
- You fulfill orders through your normal Shopify workflow — nothing new to learn

---

## Feature Your Best Products

Fundlet lets you build campaigns around specific product collections. Put your bestselling truffles, seasonal boxes, or gift assortments front and center — and give each campaign its own goal, photo, and landing page so every organization feels like the fundraiser was made just for them.

---

## Run Campaigns for Multiple Organizations at Once

Schools, sports leagues, and community groups often run fundraisers on overlapping schedules. Fundlet manages multiple active campaigns simultaneously from a single Shopify admin, each with its own goal tracker and participant list.
