Cookies are one of the most beloved fundraising products there is — easy to sell, hard to say no to, and a natural fit for schools, sports teams, and community events. With Fundlet, your cookie shop can run professional fundraising campaigns directly through your Shopify store without any paper forms or manual tracking.

---

## Replace the Cookie Dough Fundraiser with Something Better

Traditional cookie fundraisers send participants home with a paper catalog and ask them to collect orders and money by hand. Fundlet replaces that entire process with a modern online experience that's easier for participants and better for your shop:

- Participants get a unique link to share on text, email, or social media
- Supporters browse your products and check out through Shopify
- Orders are automatically tagged by campaign and participant
- You bake and fulfill through your normal workflow — no extra tools required

---

## Pre-Order Campaigns Made Simple

Many cookie shops prefer to take all orders first and bake in batches — Fundlet supports this naturally. Set a campaign end date, collect orders throughout the campaign, then fulfill everything at once when it closes. Your Shopify order management handles the rest.

---

## Give Every Organization Its Own Campaign

Schools, teams, and groups each get their own campaign page with a custom photo, goal tracker, and participant list. It feels personalized to them while you manage everything from one place in your Shopify admin.
