Nuts, trail mix, and specialty snacks have long been a staple of product fundraising — practical, shelf-stable, and appealing to a wide audience. With Fundlet, your nut or snack shop can run fundraising campaigns for schools, sports leagues, churches, and community groups directly through your Shopify store.

---

## A Natural Fit for Fundraising

Specialty snacks have a few qualities that make them ideal fundraising products: they're easy to justify as a purchase, they make great gifts, and supporters often become repeat customers once they discover your brand. Fundlet helps you turn that into a repeatable fundraising program:

- Create a campaign with your best-selling snack assortments
- Participants share their personal link and supporters order online
- Every order is tagged by campaign and participant automatically
- You fulfill through your normal Shopify workflow

---

## Bulk and Variety Packs Sell Well

Snack shops often have an advantage in fundraising because variety packs and gift boxes are natural upsells. With Fundlet, you can feature specific collections in each campaign — making it easy to push higher-value bundles that increase revenue per order for both you and the fundraising organization.

---

## Shelf-Stable Products Mean Flexible Fulfillment

Unlike baked goods or fresh flowers, nuts and snacks can be fulfilled on your own schedule. You can run longer campaigns, batch your orders, and ship on a timeline that works for your operation — all while Fundlet tracks every sale in real time.
