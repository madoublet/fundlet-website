Coffee is a natural fit for fundraising — it's something people buy regularly, it ships well, and customers love discovering a new roaster through a cause they care about. With Fundlet, your coffee shop or roastery can offer fundraising programs to schools, sports teams, churches, and local organizations directly through your Shopify store.

---

## How Coffee Shop Fundraisers Work with Fundlet

Your supporters already know how to shop online. Fundlet puts your fundraiser on your existing Shopify store, so every order goes through your normal checkout — no separate platform, no duplicate order management.

- Create a campaign for a school, team, or organization
- Set a fundraising goal and choose which products to feature
- Participants share their personal link and supporters order directly from your store
- You fulfill orders normally through Shopify; Fundlet tracks who sold what

---

## Great for Coffee Roasters

If you roast your own coffee, a fundraising program is one of the best ways to reach new customers who might never have found you otherwise. Every participant in a fundraiser becomes a micro-ambassador for your brand — and many supporters become repeat customers long after the fundraiser ends.

---

## Run Multiple Campaigns at Once

Schools, sports teams, and community groups often run fundraisers at overlapping times. Fundlet lets you manage multiple active campaigns simultaneously, each with its own goal, landing page, and participant roster — all inside your Shopify admin.
