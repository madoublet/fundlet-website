Candy and confectionery products are fundraising staples — easy to sell, easy to ship, and always popular. With Fundlet, your candy shop can run professional fundraising campaigns for schools, youth sports leagues, church groups, and community organizations, all through your existing Shopify store.

---

## Ditch the Paper Order Forms

Old-fashioned candy fundraisers rely on paper forms and manual collection. Participants carry a sheet from door to door, collect cash, and return the form to an organizer who tallies it by hand. Fundlet eliminates that entire process:

- Each participant gets a unique link to share online
- Supporters order directly from your Shopify store
- Orders are automatically tagged by campaign and participant
- You see real-time progress in your Fundlet dashboard
