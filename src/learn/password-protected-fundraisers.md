## Overview

Password protected fundraisers add a little extra privacy to your campaigns. It's easy to set up directly in the campaign editor.

---

## Step 1: Enable the Campaign Password

1. In the Fundlet app, go to **Campaigns** and open the campaign you want to protect.
2. Click **Edit Campaign** and scroll down to **Enable Campaign Password**.
3. Type in a password and hit **Save**.

---

## Step 2: Verify

Click **View Landing Page** from the campaign. You'll see a password prompt — enter your password to confirm access is working correctly.

Share the password with your participants and supporters so they can access the fundraiser.
