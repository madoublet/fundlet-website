## Overview

Once your campaign and storefront are set up on Square, the sales process is simple and works seamlessly with your existing Square store. Every sale is tracked back to the right campaign automatically.

---

## Coming up in this guide

- A supporter adds a product to cart on the campaign page
- Their purchase is attributed to the campaign at checkout
- The order appears in your Fundlet dashboard, tracked by campaign and participant
- You can review per-campaign and per-participant reporting at any time
