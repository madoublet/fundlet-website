## Overview

The countdown widget displays a timer on your campaign page showing how much time is left — a great way to create urgency and encourage supporters to buy before the campaign closes.

---

## Step 1: Set a Start and End Date on the Campaign

1. In the Fundlet app, go to **Campaigns** and open the campaign you want to update.
2. Click **Edit** and scroll down to **Campaign Visibility**.
3. Set a **begin date** and an **end date** for the campaign.
4. Hit **Save**.

The countdown timer is driven by this end date.

---

## Step 2: Enable the Countdown in the Theme Customizer

1. From the campaign, go to **More Actions → Setup Storefront → Customize**.
2. Navigate to your Fundlet campaign template.
3. Under **Apps**, open the **Banner** section.
4. Enable the **Countdown** option.
5. Hit **Save**.

---

## Step 3: Preview

Go back to the Fundlet app and click **View Storefront** to confirm the countdown timer is showing on your campaign page. The timer will display days, hours, minutes, and seconds remaining until the end date you set.
