## Overview

Payout exceptions let you set different payout amounts for different products within the same campaign. This is useful when your products have different prices or cost structures, or when you want to run a campaign across your entire store but only pay out on specific items.

---

## Step 1: Open the Campaign and Set the Payout Type

1. In the Fundlet app, go to your campaign and click **More Actions → Edit Campaign**.
2. Under **Payout Type**, select **Fixed Amount Per Item with Exceptions**.
3. Set the default payout amount — this applies to every product sold in the campaign (e.g. $25 per item).

---

## Step 2: Add Exceptions for Specific Products

Click **Select a Product** to add a payout exception. Choose a product and set a different payout amount for it. For example:

- Default payout: $25 per item
- Exception for Hawk T-Shirt: $15 per item

You can add as many exceptions as you need. Setting an exception to $0 means that product contributes no payout — useful if you want only certain items to generate a payout while everything else on your store is $0.

---

## Step 3: Save

Hit **Save** and the exceptions take effect immediately for future orders.
