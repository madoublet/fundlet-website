## Overview

A single product fundraiser targets a specific product page rather than a collection-based campaign landing page. The campaign callout — status bar, coupon code, and branding — appears directly on the product page. Setup uses a Shopify metafield to link the product to the campaign.

---

## Step 1: Create the Campaign

Create your campaign in Fundlet as normal — title, description, logo, goal, and payout. The difference: **don't assign a collection**. Since this is a single product fundraiser, you won't be using a collection.

---

## Step 2: Add the Fundlet Block to Your Product Template

In the theme customizer, go to your **Product** page template and make sure the **Fundlet Product** app block is present in the Product Information section. This block is what renders the campaign callout on the product page.

---

## Step 3: Link the Product to the Campaign via Metafield

1. In your Shopify admin, go to **Products** and open the product you want to use.
2. Scroll to the bottom and click **View All** under **Metafields**.
3. Find the Fundlet metafield — it's created automatically when the app is installed.
4. Select your campaign from the dropdown and hit **Save**.

---

## Step 4: Preview

Preview the product page. You should now see the Fundlet callout with your campaign's status bar and coupon code displayed directly on the product page, ready to go.
