## Overview

Instead of adding participants one by one, you can upload a CSV to add everyone at once. The only required columns are **name** and **email**. If you use groups, you can include an optional **group** column as well.

---

## Step 1: Prepare Your CSV

At minimum, your CSV needs two columns:

```
name,email
Alex Johnson,alex@example.com
Jordan Smith,jordan@example.com
```

If you use participant groups, add a `group` column and it will be filled in automatically during the upload.

---

## Step 2: Upload the CSV

1. In the Fundlet app, open your campaign and go to **Edit Campaign**.
2. Click **Upload Participant** and then **Add Files**.
3. Select your CSV file.

Fundlet will show a preview of the participants it found.

---

## Step 3: Add Participants and Save

Click **Add Participants**. Scroll down to confirm everyone has been added, then hit **Save**.

Each participant's referral link is generated automatically as part of the import.
