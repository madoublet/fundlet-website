## Overview

By default, Fundlet uses a popup so shoppers can quickly browse and add products without leaving your campaign landing page. But sometimes you may want to send customers directly to your existing product page instead — for example, if you have a custom workflow, a conversion-focused layout, or a product image upload step that needs the full product page experience.

Switching to your existing product page requires two small changes in the Shopify theme customizer.

---

## Step 1: Disable the Product Popup on the Collection

1. Open your Shopify theme customizer and navigate to your Fundlet campaign page.
2. In the app settings, go to the **Collection** section.
3. Find the **Use Product Popup** option and **deselect** it.
4. Save.

With this unchecked, clicking a product on the campaign landing page will navigate directly to the product page instead of opening a popup.

---

## Step 2: Add the Fundlet Block to Your Product Page

1. Still in the theme customizer, navigate to your **Product** page template.
2. In the **Product Information** section, add the **Fundlet** app block.
3. Save.

This block ensures that when a customer arrives on the product page via a Fundlet campaign link, their referral is still tracked correctly when they hit **Add to Cart**.

---

That's it. Refresh your campaign page and clicking a product will now go directly to your product page, with full Fundlet tracking intact.
