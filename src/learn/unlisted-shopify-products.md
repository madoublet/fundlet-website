## Overview

You might want to create products specifically for a fundraiser — branded items that only apply to a specific campaign, or products with a different pricing structure than your regular store. Shopify's **Unlisted** product status makes this easy: the product is sellable via direct link but doesn't show up in search or collections.

---

## Step 1: Create the Product in Shopify

1. In your Shopify admin, go to **Products** and open an existing product to duplicate, or create a new one from scratch.
2. Click **Duplicate** and give it a name.
3. Instead of setting the status to **Active**, set it to **Unlisted**.
4. Hit **Save**.

An unlisted product is sellable, but won't appear in your storefront search or any collections — perfect for a fundraiser-only item.

---

## Step 2: Add the Product to Your Campaign in Fundlet

Because the product is unlisted, it won't appear in any collection automatically — you need to add it to your campaign directly:

1. In the Fundlet app, open your campaign and go to **More Actions → Edit Campaign**.
2. Go to the **Products** section and click **Add Product**.
3. Search for the product by name and select it.
4. Hit **Save**.

The product is now part of your campaign and will appear on your campaign storefront — but nowhere else in your store.
