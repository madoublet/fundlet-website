## Overview

This is an advanced feature that lets you give different campaigns different layouts — unique text, images, videos, or any other content specific to that fundraiser. Because Fundlet uses Shopify's native tools throughout, this is done entirely in Shopify without any special app configuration.

---

## Step 1: Create Additional Templates in the Theme Customizer

1. Go to **Online Store → Themes → Customize**.
2. Navigate to the **Fundlet Campaign** template section.
3. Create additional templates (e.g. a "Rock Template", a "Spring Campaign Template") with whatever layout, images, and content you want for that specific campaign.

Each template can have different sections, different imagery, and different text — fully customized per campaign.

---

## Step 2: Assign the Template to a Campaign via Metaobjects

This part is done in Shopify, not in the Fundlet app:

1. In your Shopify admin, go to **Content → Metaobjects**.
2. Find the **Fundlet Campaign** metaobject type and locate the specific campaign you want to update.
3. Open the campaign entry and find the **Template** field.
4. Change it from the default template to your custom template.
5. Hit **Save**.

Your campaign will now use that template on the storefront.

---

## Notes

By default, all campaigns use the default template. You only need to make changes here when you want a specific campaign to look different. If you have questions about setting this up, reach out and we can help make it work for your store.
