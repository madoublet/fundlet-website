## Overview

Sharing your dashboard lets you offload work to the campaign organizer — they can check order status, export reports, and add participants themselves without needing to contact you. Organizers love it because it keeps them up to date, and you'll love it because you're not manually exporting reports every time someone asks.

---

## Step 1: Open the Campaign and Click Share Dashboard

1. In the Fundlet app, open the campaign you want to share.
2. Click **Share Dashboard** at the top of the campaign.
3. Enter an **email address** and a **PIN** for the organizer.
4. Click **Create**.

Use a strong PIN — the longer and less common, the more secure.

---

## Step 2: Copy and Send the Link

After creating, you'll see the shared dashboard link. Copy it and send it to the organizer along with their PIN.

---

## Step 3: What the Organizer Sees

When the organizer opens the link, they'll land on a login page where they enter their PIN. Once logged in, they see the full campaign dashboard:

- Total orders, items sold, total sales, and total payout
- All order line items
- An option to add participants
- An export button for reports

It's a secure, read-only-style view scoped entirely to that one campaign — no access to your Shopify admin or any other campaigns.
