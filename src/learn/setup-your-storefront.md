## Overview

Setting up your storefront uses the built-in tools already in Shopify. The whole process happens in the theme customizer and takes just a few minutes.

---

## Step 1: Open the Theme Customizer and Create a Template

1. Launch the Shopify theme customizer from **Online Store → Themes → Customize**.
2. In the customizer, go to the homepage dropdown and navigate to **Fundlet Campaign**.
3. Click **Create Template** to create a dedicated template for your campaign page.

---

## Step 2: Add the App Sections

With the new template open, click **Add Section** and add the following Fundlet app sections from the **Apps** category:

- **Banner** — displays your campaign image, name, social sharing, and progress bar.
- **Collection** — shows the products available in the campaign.
- **Participant Form** — allows new participants to onboard themselves directly from the campaign page.

---

## Step 3: Save and Preview

Click **Save**, then click **View** to preview your campaign page. You should see a fully functional page with your campaign image, campaign name, social sharing buttons, a progress bar, and your products — ready to go.

That's it. Your storefront is set up and ready to host your Fundlet campaign.
