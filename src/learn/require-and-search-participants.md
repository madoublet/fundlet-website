## Overview

The Fundlet message block has two advanced features: a searchable participant dropdown and a required participant selection. This is useful when you have a lot of participants and want to make sure every order is attributed to someone.

---

## Step 1: Enable Searchable Participants

1. In the Shopify theme customizer, open your campaign storefront and click the **Message** block.
2. Change the participant selector to **Searchable Dropdown**.
3. Hit **Save**.

Shoppers can now type a name to filter the participant list — useful when you have many participants to scroll through.

---

## Step 2: Require a Participant Selection

1. In the theme customizer, click the **Message** block.
2. Under the message settings, click **Require Selection**.
3. Hit **Save**.

When require selection is on, the banner and collection block are hidden until a participant is chosen. Once a shopper selects a participant and clicks **View**, they're taken to that participant's page where the products and banner appear.

This is an easy way to make sure every purchase is tied to a specific participant.
