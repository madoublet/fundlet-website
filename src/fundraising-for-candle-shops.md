Candles are one of the most successful fundraising products going — they have broad appeal, feel like a real gift, and customers are happy to pay a premium for something they'll actually use. With Fundlet, your candle shop can offer fundraising programs to schools, PTAs, sports teams, and community organizations directly through your Shopify store.

---

## Why Candles Work So Well for Fundraising

Unlike discount products that feel like an obligation purchase, candles are something people genuinely want. That means higher sell-through rates for participants and more revenue per order for your organization. With Fundlet, you can:

- Create campaigns around your most giftable scents and collections
- Set a fundraising goal and watch progress in real time
- Give participants a personal link to share — no catalog carrying required
- Receive all orders through your normal Shopify checkout and fulfillment

---

## Perfect for Seasonal Campaigns

Candle shops have a natural advantage for fall and holiday fundraising — two of the biggest fundraising seasons of the year. Fundlet makes it easy to spin up a campaign quickly so you can take advantage of seasonal demand when it peaks.

---

## No Extra Platform to Manage

Everything runs inside Shopify. There's no separate fundraising portal to log into, no duplicate order data to reconcile, and no new system to learn. Fundlet adds fundraising on top of what you already have.
